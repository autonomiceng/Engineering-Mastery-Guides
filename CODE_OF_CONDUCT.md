# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participation in this repository should remain professional, direct, and respectful.
Technical disagreement is expected. Personal attacks, harassment, discrimination, and bad-faith behavior are not acceptable.

## Expected Behavior

- Critique ideas, code, and claims rather than people
- State technical objections clearly and concretely
- Disclose uncertainty instead of guessing
- Prefer reproducible evidence over rhetoric

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or intimidation
- Discriminatory or abusive language
- Deliberate misrepresentation of results or review status
- Spam, trolling, or disruptive conduct

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior reduces the repository's technical quality or safety.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainer rather than escalating them in public threads when possible.
